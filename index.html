What can fast food tell us about Sydney?

Sydney is a diverse city, but there is nothing more universal than fast food. But from Bondi to Mt Druitt, the greasiness, price, availability, marketing and taste of fast food changes. So what can this tell us about Sydney-siders? A lot, it turns out.

My interest began when Twitter user @THE_REAL_BIGJEZ tweeted this a map of Sydney and the "Red Rooster line" dividing western Sydney from the east.
